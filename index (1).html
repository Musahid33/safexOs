⚠️ Emergency Hone Par Kya Karein

1
Shant Rahein: Ghabrana nahi, pehle situation ko samjhein aur control karein.
2
Immediate Report Karein: Apne supervisor ya safety officer ko turant soochit karein.
3
Area Khali Karein: Designated emergency exit se safe zone mein jayein.
4
PPE Use Karein: Emergency ke hisaab se proper PPE pehnein (helmet, mask, gloves).
5
Doosron Ki Madad Karein: Zakhmi logon ko safe jagah le jaayein aur medical help bulayein.
